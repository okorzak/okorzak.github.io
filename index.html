Storyteller and Journalist

Owen Korzak is a current undergraduate student at the University of Maryland Philip Merrill College of Journalism, where he is interested in sports broadcasting. He grew up in Fairfax County, VA, where he attended Fairfax High School. At Fairfax, Owen served as the Sports & Activites and Executive Editor for FAIR FACTS, a student-led, quartly publication. Since arriving in Maryland, Owen has joined a plethera of oraganizations inlcudling Terrapin Sports Central, B1G+, WMUC and Capital News Service.
